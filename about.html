About The Kitchen

Our home is located in the outskirts of Bangalore, where we invite guests to celebrate life and share sundary blends of modern and traditional Italian cuisine.Our goal at The Kitchen is to unify our guests cultures and spirits,with our own,expressing passion and generosity in the cuisine we share.

    We believe in creating interesting and unique places to enjoy our simple and great tasting “Real Italian” food. Sometimes we pop up in shopping centres, sometimes in a great shopping strip. Either way we’ll make sure you feel like you’re in our home and ready for some of our great, simple food.

The Kitchen is one of the country’s most celebrated restaurants; The Kitchen is an organic space reflective of Ross Gellers nature inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place in the dress circle of Bangalore.
